00 — Philosophy

Lean Startup, Applied

Don't build a perfect brand before anyone cares. Ship the minimum, learn from real humans, and double down on what works. Every post is a hypothesis. Every comment is data.

🧱
Build (MVP First)
Your MVP is your first 9 posts and a WhatsApp group with 10 people. No website needed yet. No paid ads. Just content and conversation.
📊
Measure (Real Signals)
Track saves, shares, replies, and WhatsApp responses — not just likes. These are the metrics that prove real resonance, not vanity.
🔁
Learn (Fast Loops)
Every 2 weeks: review data, drop what didn't land, amplify what did. At Day 30 and Day 60, make a formal pivot-or-persevere decision.

🧪 First MVP Product: Free "Lab Zero" Session

What is Lab Zero?

A free, 60-minute Zoom/Google Meet session where 15–20 participants experience one self-leadership exercise together. No pitch. No product. Just value. This is your riskiest assumption to test: will people show up?

How to promote it
  • → IG Countdown sticker in Stories 1 week before
  • → TikTok: "I'm opening my lab for free — here's why"
  • → WA group: personal invite with link to sign-up form
  • → After event: collect feedback via Google Form (5 questions)

⚗️ First Paid Offer: "Lab One" Cohort Program

What It Is

A 4-week online cohort. 20 people max. Weekly 90-min Zoom sessions + WhatsApp accountability pod. Focused on one pillar: Self-Awareness.

MVP Price
RM25

Low price = high signup rate = rich feedback. Raise price in Cohort 2 based on demand.

Lean Logic

20 paying users at any price proves willingness to pay — more valuable than 200 free users. This is your pivot point data.

✅ Persevere Signals

  • Posts consistently get 3%+ save rate
  • WA group members reply without prompting
  • Lab Zero session had 10+ attendees
  • People are DM-ing YOU asking for more
  • At least 1 TikTok broke through organically
  • Someone says "this is exactly what I needed"

⚠️ Pivot Signals

  • Save rate stuck below 1% after 30 posts
  • WA group is silent — prompts get no response
  • Lab Zero got fewer than 5 attendees
  • Zero DMs expressing interest in paid offer
  • Same content formats keep underperforming
  • Audience skews differently than expected

04 — Community Engine

WhatsApp Community Playbook

WhatsApp is your retention engine and your feedback lab. It's where strangers become believers. Treat it like a product, not a group chat.

Group Structure

Start with one intimate group. Scale into a WhatsApp Community with sub-groups as you grow. Think: neighbourhood → village → city.

Growth Stages
Days 1–30: "Beta Lab" — 1 group, 30 people, invite only
Days 31–60: Add announcement channel, peer pairings
Days 61–90: Community with sub-groups (Cohort, General, Mentors)

Community Rules — "The Lab Code"

🔬
Be the experiment
Share your real experiences. Vulnerability is the currency here.
🚫
No spam or promotions
This is a growth space, not a marketplace.
🤝
Ask before you give advice
Check if someone wants a listener or a problem solver first.
🎯
Show up, even small
One emoji reaction counts. Presence matters more than words.
